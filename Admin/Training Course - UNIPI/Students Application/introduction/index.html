    Introduzione

    Since the course will be designed for students and industrial players, each university will active a promotion process using the University channels. Through communication tools such as brochure, posters, etc., the activation of the course will be channeled on the University website and showcases, on the project website, but also on the several communication platform of the local environment, in order to connect with the industrial players.

    In order to participate, the candidate will apply for the course following a selection procedure. The general idea is the following:

    • Compile the Mandatory Application Form
    • Evaluation of the candidate
    • Communication to the candidate the results of the selection process
    • Compile the Optional Application Form
